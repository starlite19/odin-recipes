Iced Matcha Latte

Description

A simple recipe that takes minutes to make, but keeps you energized all day long.

Ingredients

  • Matcha
  • Hot water
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk
  • Sweetener (sugar, syrup, etc)

Steps

  1. Add ice cubes, milk and sweetener into a glass cup
  2. Whisk matcha and hot water until no lumps remain. Matcha should smooth and a little foamy
  3. Pour the matcha mixture into the milk glass
