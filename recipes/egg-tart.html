Egg Tart

Description

An easy recipe to make classic Hong Kong style egg tarts!

Ingredients

  • 1 and half cups of water
  • 3 quarter cup of white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 quarter cup of evaporated milk
  • 24 (3 inch) unbaked tart shells

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  2. Put water and white sugar into a sauce pan and cook until the sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and cool to room temperature
  3. Beat the eggs in a large bowl; add evaporated milk and continue beating. Pour the cooled sugar water and mix until well combined
  4. Place tart shells on a baking sheet. Pour the filling through a sieve and fill the tart shells
  5. Bake until the filling has puffed a little bit. Usually about 20 minutes
